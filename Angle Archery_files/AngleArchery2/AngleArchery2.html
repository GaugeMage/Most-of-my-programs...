Here are the Rules:

Press the W key once to shoot the bow.

Press the A key to aim left.

Press the D key to aim right.

Press the Space key to put the arrow back in the bow

If you hit a target you will be asked a certain amount of question(s).

If you hit a red target you will be asked 1 question.

If you hit a gold, blue, or green target you will be asked 1-3 questions.

If you get more than 100 points you will go to level 2

For each 100 points you get the higher the level.

When you enter your name the game will start.

You will have 60 seconds to score

Only 60 seconds to get to level 5!

Key

  • Red Targets = 1 point
  • Gold Targets = 20 - 60 points and Slo-Mo!
  • Blue Targets = 0 - 100 points!
  • Green Targets = 20 - 60 points and Double Shot!
  • Keep in mind that this is still quite buggy so don't expect a lot.
